[RegulaIDo](https://www.pharos-labs.com/regulaido/) is an AI-powered platform built for medical writing teams working on regulatory submissions, systematic literature reviews, and market access dossiers.

The core feature is an agentic AI chat with full traceability: users upload clinical documents and ask questions, and the AI responds with citations to exact source passages — eliminating hallucinations in a domain where accuracy is non-negotiable.

Built for regulated environments with ISO 27001 certification, Azure AD SSO, full audit trails, and validation support documentation designed for GxP processes. Users report efficiency gains up to 97% on complex medical writing tasks while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Part of [Pharos Labs](https://www.pharos-labs.com), an AI company focused on medical writing and healthcare applications.
