[PlazmaPunk](https://plazmapunk.com) turns any song into an AI-generated music video. Users upload an MP3 or WAV, choose from styles like anime, cyberpunk, film noir, or oil painting, and receive a rendered video within minutes.

Built with a generative AI pipeline that synchronizes visual transformations to audio features — beat detection drives scene transitions, spectral energy shapes the visual intensity, and style transfer networks handle the aesthetic. The platform supports a Scene Editor and Story Director for fine-grained creative control.

Currently serving creators on a freemium model with tiers from free (20 daily credits) to Producer (3,000 daily credits). An API is available for developers who want to integrate video generation into their own products.
