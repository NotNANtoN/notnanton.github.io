AI Researcher and Entrepreneur driven by the quest for adaptive intelligence. My journey began watching AI master Atari games and Go, leading to top-of-class degrees in AI and real-world impact through published medical AI research.

At [Pharos Labs](https://pharoslabs.de), I lead the development of Regulaido, revolutionizing medical writing through intelligent document processing. Previously co-founded [AdaLab](https://adalab.ai), where our critical care prediction work made it to the BRAIN journal, and we created Plazmapunk, pushing boundaries in AI-powered music video generation.

Fascinated by the future of intelligence: combining large language models with reinforcement learning, exploring mathematical formulations of curiosity, and building modular systems that can truly learn from their environment. Working towards AI that doesn't just process information, but understands and adapts to our world.
